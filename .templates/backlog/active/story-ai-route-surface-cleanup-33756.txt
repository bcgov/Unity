Title
{AI} Clean Up AI Route Surface and Proxy Call Sites

Description
The AI route surface still exposes both the new `/api/app/ai/*` endpoints and older Grant Manager AI queue/status entry points, which leaves the UI and generated proxies split across two surfaces. The AI generation service also does not expose a stable `GetStatus` endpoint, so UI polling still depends on the older Grant Manager path.

Repro Steps
1. Open the Grant Manager AI UI and inspect the generated proxy calls for AI generation, analysis, scoring, or attachment summary flows.
2. Note that some call sites still use `grantApplication.*` queue/status methods.
3. Inspect `IGrantApplicationAppService` and `IAIGenerationAppService`.
4. Observe that the old Grant Manager AI queue/status methods still exist, while the new AI generation service does not expose a stable `GetStatus` route.
